Hi. We are CORE.

We spread the love for entrepreneurship among NUS students.

Billed as the hub of entrepreneurship, CORE connects student and entrepreneurs alike to the start-up network, hoping to spread entrepreneurship and inspire more start-ups. We also emphasize on member development, making sure that every one of our members gain skills and knowledge for their future endeavours.

Events

We come up with initiatives that help to expose our audience to entrepreneurship. Here, we challenge you to set your minds free and think of creative ways to do so! Beyond the sheer satisfaction of a packed auditorium, you will get to learn from professionals too!

LINK

Being the practical arm of NES, we get first-hand information on resources such as mentors and funding and pass them onto our participants. Join us and you will get a chance to collaborate with exciting start-ups. Who knows, you might even be inspired to have one!

Marketing & IT

At MIT, we synergise traditional and advanced media to create dynamic and vibrant publicity materials. We undertake projects that frequently take us into the realms of Technopreneurship. We bring you our design skills, coding experience and passion.

Sponsorship

We provide essential ingredient for the society to function - MONEY! With sponsorship pitches among the hardest to pitch, you will master the way to charm sponsors, understand value propositions and get an "in" with these vital contacts!

Talent Management

At TM, we work together to create a sense of belonging and deveop the most important resource in NES: our members. We recruit talents and keep them (and us) happy!
