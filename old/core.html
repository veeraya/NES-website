Hi. We are CORE.

We spread the love for entrepreneurship among NUS students.

Events

We come up with initiatives that help to expose our audience to entrepreneurship. Here, we challenge you to set your minds free and think of creative ways to do so! We equip budding entrepreneurs with essential skills through workshops, seminars, hand-on activities and networking sessions.

LINK (Biz Development)

Being the practical arm of NES, we get first-hand information on resources such as mentors and funding and pass them onto our participants. It aims to privde platforms and resources for budding entrepreneurs to explore, develop and execute ideas, through mentorships, networking, workshop and consultation.

Marketing & IT

At MIT, we make use of traditional and advanced media to create dynamic and vibrant publicity materials. We undertake projects that frequently take us into the realms of Technopreneurship. We bring you our design skills, coding experience and passion. (and yes, this website.)

Talent Management

The TM department focuses on designing leadership development and aptitude enrichment programs in addition to offering an exciting and fun culture in this professionally organised society.

One of the highlights is a 2-week overseas leadership development trip to Silicon Valley for upcoming NES leaders.

Sponsorship

We provide essential ingredient for the society to function - MONEY! With sponsorship pitches among the hardest to pitch, you will master the way to charm sponsors, understand value propositions and get an "in" with these vital contacts!
